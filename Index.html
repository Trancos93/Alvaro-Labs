How to Avoid Procrastination - Get things done

So how do we deal with one of the worst sins of modern society

Customize and plan your day

Our brains spend around 1 fourth of our body's total energy. It's the reason why we iare more effective early in the morning. Imagine our brains, full of willpower at the begining of the day, it would only make sense to do the harder tasks at this time of the day, when we are at our best, leaving for the end of the day, the harder ones, when we are at our worst. Our brains' energy is not infinite, so managing it wisely is key to being productive, Understanding your golder hours is a must if your goal is to start procrastinating less and getting things done.

Take care of your sleep

Tied with the above, if we want our brains to be awake and productive, we need to care more about our sleep. Sleep depravation is the single worst pandemic that affects modern society, and it's the single most powerfull activity you can work on to hack your performance in every other aspect of your life. Good sleep improves concentration, memory, creativity, fisical performance, coordination, the list just goes on and on... Also, you'll have more of that energy we previously talked about.
